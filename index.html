Key features
01

Listen to music

Users can stream music directly on the EchoFi platform. For every verified listen, users earn ECHO tokens or points, depending on the track's engagement model.

02

Refer friend

Users receive a unique referral link to invite others to the platform. When new users sign up and engage (e.g., listen to music, stake, or make purchases), the referrer earns a percentage of their activity.

03

Stake ECHO

Users can lock (stake) their ECHO tokens for a defined period to earn staking rewards. The longer the staking duration, the higher the APY (Annual Percentage Yield).

04

Share your review

Users who leave meaningful reviews or feedback on songs, albums, or the platform itself can earn ECHO rewards.

01

What do I need to get started with EchoFi?

You just need to create a free account using your email. To withdraw rewards or trade NFTs, connect a Web3 wallet like MetaMask or TON for full access to EchoFi features.

02

How does listening to music earn me rewards?

When you stream songs on EchoFi, you earn ECHO tokens based on your listening activity. The more you listen, the more rewards you collect verified with our Proof-of-Listening system.

03

How to invite friends and get rewards?

Share your unique referral link with friends. Once they sign up and start listening, you instantly receive ECHO token rewards, with additional milestone bonuses as your network grows.

03

How can artists publish and earn with EchoFi?

Artists can upload music as NFTs, earning directly from streams, NFT sales, and royalties. EchoFi also supports artist promotion, merchandise sales, and virtual concerts for extra income.

05

What is the EchoFi Music Box?

The Music Box is a mystery NFT box containing rewards like concert tickets, signed merchandise, VIP Power Cards, and bonus Echo tokens offering real utility and collectible value.

06

Is EchoFi safe to use?

Yes. EchoFi is built on its own secure blockchain (Echo Chain) with fast transactions and low fees. All activities and rewards are recorded transparently on-chain for full user security.
